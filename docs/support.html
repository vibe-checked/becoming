Common questions

How does a session work?

Choose one of 8 themes and a duration (3, 5, 10, or 15 minutes). The app fills your screen with slowly shifting gradient visuals while affirmations appear as floating text cards every 15 seconds. Each affirmation is also read aloud by your device's text-to-speech engine over ambient music. When the timer ends, you're asked to log how you feel.

Can I skip an affirmation?

In the current version, affirmations cycle automatically every 15 seconds. You can stop the session at any time using the ✕ button in the top-right corner. Controls for skipping, muting, and favoriting are coming in a future update.

Where is my data stored?

All data — your theme preference, session duration, and reflection journal — is stored locally on your device using standard app storage. Nothing is sent to any server. If you delete the app, your data is removed. If you have iCloud Backup enabled, your device may include the app's storage in its system backup.

Can I add my own affirmations?

Not yet — custom affirmations are planned for a future update. For now, each theme includes 30 handcrafted affirmations that are shuffled each session so the order stays fresh.

The voice sounds robotic — can I change it?

Becoming uses your device's built-in text-to-speech engine. The voice quality depends on your iOS version and which voices you have downloaded. For the best experience, go to Settings → Accessibility → Spoken Content → Voices → English and download an "Enhanced" or "Premium" voice.

Can I use it offline?

Yes — Becoming works entirely offline. No internet connection is required at any point. All affirmations, visuals, and audio are bundled with the app.

Will it come to Android?

Becoming is built on Expo and runs from a single codebase on iOS, Android, and web. The first public release focuses on iOS via the App Store. Android will follow once iOS is stable.

Reporting accessibility issues

Accessibility feedback is taken seriously. If something is hard to read, tap, or hear, please email with details — device, OS version, and an example — and I'll work with you on a fix.

Response time

I aim to reply to every email within three business days. If you haven't heard back in a week, please send a follow-up — it almost always means the original got buried.

Becoming is a one-developer indie project. Your feedback genuinely shapes what gets built next. Thank you for using it.
